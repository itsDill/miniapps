About IoWidgets

IoT Made Simple — Honest reviews for real people

Our Mission

Smart home technology shouldn't be confusing. At IoWidgets, we believe everyone deserves clear, honest information to make smart buying decisions — without needing an engineering degree.

We test every device ourselves, in real homes, with real-world usage. No sponsored fluff. No manufacturer bias. Just straightforward reviews that help you choose the right gear for your needs and budget.

What Makes Us Different

🔬 Real Testing, Real Results

We don't just read spec sheets. Every product we review is tested in actual homes for at least 2-4 weeks. We check reliability, app experience, compatibility with Alexa/Google/HomeKit, and long-term performance.

📊 Data-Driven Ratings

Our scoring system weighs multiple factors: setup ease (15%), daily usability (25%), reliability (25%), features (20%), and value for money (15%). No arbitrary scores — every rating is backed by our testing data.

🎯 Beginner-Friendly Approach

Technical jargon doesn't help anyone. We explain everything in plain language so you understand exactly what you're buying, whether it's your first smart plug or your tenth security camera.

💰 Transparent Affiliate Model

Yes, we earn commissions from Amazon and other retailers when you buy through our links. This is how we fund our testing and keep the site free. But here's our promise: our recommendations are always based on merit, not commission rates. We've recommended cheaper products over expensive ones countless times when they performed better.

Our Testing Lab

Our test environment includes:

  • Multiple Wi-Fi setups (mesh, single router, congested networks)
  • All major voice assistants (Alexa, Google Assistant, Siri/HomeKit)
  • Smart home hubs (SmartThings, Hubitat, Home Assistant)
  • Real power monitoring equipment
  • Security testing for cameras and locks
