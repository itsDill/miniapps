About HomeSense

We're on a mission to make smart home monitoring accessible, affordable, and easy for everyone.

Our Story

HomeSense was born from a simple frustration: existing smart home platforms were either too expensive, too complicated, or locked into proprietary ecosystems. We believed there had to be a better way.

Founded in 2024, our team set out to build an open, flexible platform that works with any IoT sensor or smart device. We wanted to empower homeowners to take control of their living spaces‚Äîwhether that means saving energy, enhancing security, or simply making life more convenient.

Today, HomeSense serves thousands of homes worldwide, from small apartments to large estates. We've expanded our vision to include AgriSense, bringing the same powerful monitoring capabilities to farmers and agricultural operations.

Our Mission

We believe that smart technology should work for everyone‚Äînot just tech enthusiasts with unlimited budgets. Our mission is to democratize smart home monitoring by providing powerful, intuitive tools that anyone can use.

We're committed to building technology that's open, interoperable, and privacy-respecting. Your data belongs to you, and our platform is designed to give you complete control over how it's collected, stored, and used.
