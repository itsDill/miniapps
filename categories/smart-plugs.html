How to Choose a Smart Plug in 2026: Matter, Thread & WiFi Explained

Wondering which smart plug works with your setup? Here's what apartment renters and homeowners need to know:

🔌 What Can You Automate With a Smart Plug?

  • Lamps & string lights — voice control or sunset schedules
  • Coffee makers & fans — morning routines via Alexa or Siri
  • Monitor energy usage — find power-hungry devices (KP125M, Eve Energy)
  • Vacation mode — random on/off to simulate presence
  • Control from anywhere — forgot to turn off the iron?

🎯 Matter vs WiFi vs Thread: Which Protocol Do You Need?

  • WiFi plugs (Kasa EP25, Wyze, Amazon Basics): Work directly with your router, no hub needed. Best for most people.
  • Matter-certified (KP125M, Eve Energy, Meross): Universal standard that works across Apple Home, Google, Alexa & SmartThings. Future-proof choice.
  • Thread plugs (Eve Energy): Low-power mesh network. Requires Thread border router (HomePod Mini, Apple TV 4K, or Echo). Best for Apple-heavy homes.

🍎 Which Smart Plug Works With Apple HomeKit?

  • Native HomeKit: Eve Energy, Meross Smart Plug Mini
  • Matter (works with HomeKit): Kasa KP125M, Kasa EP25
  • Alexa only: Amazon Basics Smart Plug (no HomeKit)

⚠️ What NOT to Plug Into a Smart Plug

  • Space heaters (fire hazard)
  • High-wattage appliances exceeding plug rating
  • Devices that need to run continuously (refrigerators)
  • Medical equipment
