Smart Plug Buying Guide

Not sure which smart plug is right for you? Here's what to consider:

🔌 What Can You Do With a Smart Plug?

  • Control lamps, fans, and appliances with your voice or phone
  • Set schedules to turn devices on/off automatically
  • Monitor energy usage and reduce electricity bills
  • Create automations (e.g., turn on lights at sunset)
  • Control devices remotely when away from home

🎯 Key Features to Consider

  • Voice Assistant Support: Make sure it works with your smart speaker (Alexa, Google, Siri)
  • Matter Support: Future-proof standard that works across all ecosystems
  • Energy Monitoring: Track power usage of connected devices
  • Size: Compact plugs don't block adjacent outlets
  • Max Load: Check the wattage limit for high-power devices

⚠️ What NOT to Plug In

  • Space heaters (fire hazard)
  • High-wattage appliances exceeding plug rating
  • Devices that need to run continuously (refrigerators)
  • Medical equipment
