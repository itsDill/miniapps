Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response? +
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, our premium support subscribers receive priority responses within 4 hours.
Do you offer phone support? +
Phone support is available for Enterprise plan subscribers. For other plans, we offer email and live chat support during business hours.
Can I schedule a product demo? +
Absolutely! Select "Sales & Pricing" as the subject in the contact form and mention that you'd like a demo. Our team will reach out to schedule a personalized walkthrough of the platform.
I'm interested in a partnership. Who should I contact? +
For partnership inquiries, please email us at partners@homesense.io or select "Partnership Opportunities" in the contact form.
How can I report a bug or security issue? +
For bug reports, please use "Technical Support" in the subject line and include as much detail as possible. For security-related issues, please email security@homesense.io directly.
