Understanding Your Paycheck

Your take-home pay is what you actually receive after taxes and deductions. This calculator provides estimates - actual amounts may vary based on your location, filing status, and specific deductions.

Common Deductions Include:

  • Federal Income Tax: Progressive tax based on income brackets
  • State Income Tax: Varies by state (0-13%)
  • FICA: Social Security (6.2%) + Medicare (1.45%)
  • Health Insurance: Premiums for medical, dental, vision
  • Retirement Contributions: 401(k), IRA contributions
  • Other: Life insurance, FSA, HSA, garnishments

Salary Negotiation Tips:

  • Always negotiate in annual salary terms, not hourly rates
  • Research market rates for your position and location
  • Consider total compensation including benefits and bonuses
  • Know your minimum acceptable salary before negotiating
  • Let the employer make the first offer when possible
